Honouring Our Physics Teacher

About our Teacher

Our middle school physics teacher, Lakshmi S, made learning fun and engaging for all of us. Her passion for physics turned even difficult topics into interesting lessons. She also went out of her way to help us with math, even though it wasn't required. Loved by all her students, she created a classroom where curiosity and joy in learning thrived. Her dedication and innovative teaching left a lasting impact on our education.

To learn more about Lakshmi and her incredible teaching journey, read ahead.

Early Life

    Born on November 14, 1979, which coincides with Children's Day in India, Lakshmi's early life was filled with a love for children that would later inspire her career in teaching. She excelled in her academic journey, completing her schooling with distinction from Toc-H public School, Ernakulam. She pursued her higher education at the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology in Kerala, where she earned a degree in Civil Engineering. Her academic achievements laid a solid foundation for her future endeavors.

Teaching career

    After completing her engineering degree, Lakshmi made a bold choice to follow her passion for teaching, turning down a lucrative job offer from Infosys. She began her teaching career at Ryan International School in Pune, where she honed her skills and shared her love for learning with her students. Her journey then took her to Chennai, where she taught Class 11 and 12 students at Asan Memorial School, enriching their academic lives. Later, she transitioned to teaching middle school students at DAV Matriculation School, where she continued to inspire young minds. Currently, Lakshmi has come full circle and is back in Kochi, her hometown, teaching at GEMS Modern Academy.

Life Lately

Currently, she's the Vice Principal - Primary and the Academic Systems Co-ordinator at GEMS Modern Academy, Smart city, Kochi.
